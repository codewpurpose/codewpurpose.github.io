East Bay SPCA

The East Bay SPCA has been saving lost and abandoned animals since 1874. They are an independent, nonprofit organization serving Alameda and Contra Costa Counties. They operate two Adoption and Education Centers, two Spay/Neuter Surgery Centers, and a full-service Veterinary Clinic. Please leave a comment, "I Came From CodeWithPurpose". In this way, we can track how many donations have been made to East Bay SPCA through our organization!
